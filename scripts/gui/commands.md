# Drive
Enter drive mode. 

## From here the arrow keys will:
- drive forward 15 cm
- drive backwards 15 cm
- turn left 10 deg 
- turn right 10 deg

## Special commands:
- hit 'm' to run a scan
- hit 'f' to drive forward 1 m
- hit 'l' to turn left 90 degrees
- hit 'r' to turn right 90 degress
- hit 'p' to play music

# Scan
Run a CyBOT scan

# Calibrate
Calibrate one of the following: servo, turning, adc
*Warning, the turning calibration is pretty sketch*

# Quit
Quits the program
